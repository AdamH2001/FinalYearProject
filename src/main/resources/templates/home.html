Welcome to After School Club

Welcome to our website! This is your one-stop destination for booking your child into all our exciting after school clubs.

At After School Club we understand how important it is to provide a safe, enjoyable and stimulating environment for your child outside of school hours. We have a wide range of engaging and enriching activities available including sports, team games, art and craft, music and dance. It’s easy to find the perfect club to help you child develop new skills, make friends and have fun.

If you need more information, please contact the school office on 01256 888888 or email us on admin@afterschoolclub.com

Registration

The entire registration process is managed efficiently through our website. Both the new parent user and child registration forms are completed on-line. You can also manage your account and add funds using our on-line payment system.

Once this simple process is completed you are ready to book your child’s place at any of our exciting clubs.

Booking

The full range of amazing clubs on offer is available to view under the Club Timetable. Information provided also includes activity description, date, start time, end time, age group suitability and price.

Booking your child into a session is so effortless and can be actioned with a few clicks. Regular blocked bookings can also be made.

Refreshments

We offer a full range of healthy, nutritional and appetising food and drink options from a hearty and filling breakfast to energising snacks at our after school clubs.

Your food choices are selected at the time of booking and this gives you the option of choosing at home with your child. We will take into account any dietary requirements and food allergies.
